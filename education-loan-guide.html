Loan guide

Education loan EMI: plan beyond the course fee

An education loan can cover a meaningful opportunity, but repayment terms and moratorium interest deserve close attention.

Understand the moratorium period

Some loans defer full EMI payments while you study and for a period after the course. Interest may still accrue during that time. Confirm whether it is paid as it accrues or added to the outstanding amount.

Estimate the repayment amount

Calculate using the expected balance at repayment start, the lender's rate and the chosen tenure. If the final disbursement amount is uncertain, test a conservative higher amount in the calculator.

Keep documents and terms organised

  • Save the sanction letter and repayment schedule.
  • Record each disbursement and fee.
  • Know any co-applicant obligations.
  • Ask what changes if the course duration changes.
