About us

Built by people who were tired of confusing loan quotes

Finura started as a simple observation: most loan calculators online either bury you in ads, demand a phone number before showing a single number, or hide the total cost of borrowing behind a friendly-looking monthly figure. We built something plainer.

Our mission

To give anyone comparing a loan the same clarity a financially literate friend would give them — for free, without a sales pitch, and without collecting personal data to do it.

Our vision

A future where checking whether a loan offer is fair is as easy as checking a weather forecast: instant, free, and something you don't need to be a finance professional to understand.

Why trust us

  • Standard formula, no black box. Our calculator uses the same reducing-balance EMI formula lenders use, laid out in full in our how EMI is calculated guide, so you can verify it yourself.
  • No data collection on the calculator. All figures you enter are processed locally in your browser and never transmitted anywhere.
  • We are not a lender. We don't sell loans, take referral fees from lenders, or push you toward any particular financial product.
  • Content is written and reviewed for accuracy, not generated to rank for keywords. See our editorial policy below.

Editorial policy

Every guide on this site is written to explain a real concept a borrower needs to understand, using plain language and worked examples wherever possible. We avoid vague generalities and financial jargon without explanation. Articles are reviewed periodically for accuracy as interest rate environments and lending practices change.

Content standards

We do not provide personalized financial advice, and nothing on this site should be read as a recommendation to take a specific loan or lender. Figures used in examples are illustrative. Always confirm final terms directly with your lender before signing any loan agreement. Read our full disclaimer.

Have a question or spotted something that needs correcting? Get in touch — we read every message.
