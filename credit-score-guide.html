Credit basics

Understanding your credit score

Your credit history gives lenders context, but it is only one part of an approval and pricing decision.

What lenders may consider

Lenders can consider your repayment history, existing obligations, income, requested loan amount and their own policy. A score is not a guarantee of approval or a particular rate.

Build healthy credit habits

  • Pay every bill and EMI on time.
  • Borrow only what you can comfortably repay.
  • Review your credit report for errors through the appropriate provider.
  • Avoid making many credit applications in a short period unless necessary.

Keep your loan affordable

Before applying, use the EMI calculator to test the payment against your regular expenses and savings goals. On-time repayment is easier when the loan fits your budget.
