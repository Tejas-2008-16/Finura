Loan guide

Personal loan EMI: borrow with a clear repayment plan

Personal loans are flexible, but their rates are often higher than secured loans. Compare the full cost, not only the advertised EMI.

Why the rate matters more

Because there is usually no asset pledged as security, personal-loan rates may be higher. A small rate difference can have a meaningful effect on a short or medium-term loan. Use your actual quoted rate in the EMI calculator.

Borrow only what you need

Build a simple cost estimate and finance only the gap. A larger loan increases both the EMI and the interest you pay, regardless of how easily it is approved.

Review the offer document

  • Annualised interest rate and repayment tenure.
  • Processing fee and tax on the fee.
  • Late-payment and prepayment conditions.
  • Whether the rate or repayment schedule can change.

Protect your monthly cash flow

Leave room for essential expenses and savings. If the EMI makes your budget too tight, reconsider the amount or wait until the expense can be met more safely.
