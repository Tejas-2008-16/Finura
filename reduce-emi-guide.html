Borrowing strategy

How to reduce your EMI responsibly

There are several ways to lower a monthly payment. Each changes either the loan amount, rate or repayment period—and each has a trade-off.

Make a larger down payment

Borrowing less lowers the EMI and total interest. Do not use funds needed for emergencies or essential costs just to reduce the payment.

Negotiate the interest rate

A lower rate reduces both EMI and total cost. Compare like-for-like offers and consider every fee, not simply a headline rate.

Extend the tenure carefully

Extending the tenure can reduce the EMI, but normally increases total interest. Use the calculator to view the exact trade-off.

Prepay when it makes sense

An eligible part-prepayment can reduce future interest. Check conditions and keep enough liquidity before committing surplus cash.
