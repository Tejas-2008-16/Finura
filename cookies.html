Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This page explains how Finura uses cookies and similar technologies.

What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as preferences or, for advertising cookies, your browsing activity across sites.

How we use cookies

TypePurpose
EssentialBasic site functionality, such as remembering your navigation menu state.
AnalyticsUnderstanding aggregate, anonymized traffic patterns to improve the site.
AdvertisingIf ads are displayed on this site, our ad partners may use cookies to serve relevant advertising and measure performance.

Our EMI calculators themselves do not use cookies — all calculations happen locally in your browser and are never stored.

Managing cookies

Most browsers let you block or delete cookies through their settings. Blocking cookies may affect some site functionality but will not affect your ability to use our calculators.

Third-party cookies

Where this site displays third-party advertising, those providers may set their own cookies subject to their own privacy policies, which we do not control.
