Cookie Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026. Review how we use tracking tokens to manage layouts, theme states, and advertising services.

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are and how we use them on Finura (https://finura-calculator.com). You should read this policy to understand what cookies we use, the information we collect using cookies, and how that information is used.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information to the owners of the website.

2. How We Use Cookies

We classify our cookie usage into three primary categories:
• Essential Cookies: These are cookies required for technical operations. For example, we use local storage parameters to remember your light/dark mode theme choices and your cookie consent selection. These do not gather marketing data.
• Advertising & Target Cookies: We integrate third-party vendors (such as Google AdSense) who place cookies in your browser to evaluate advertising campaign yields and deliver targeted advertisements that align with your interest profile.
• Analytics & Performance Cookies: These allow us to measure general traffic patterns (like which blog guides are read most often) so we can continuously improve our educational content.

3. Third-Party Vendor Privacy

Google and other ad networks utilize cookies to deliver ads on Finura. You can opt out of personalized marketing cookies by adjusting your browser settings or visiting the official Google Ads settings page. Disabling cookies will not prevent you from using our calculator engine, as all calculation code runs locally on your browser.

4. Controlling Your Preferences

Most web browsers allow you to block cookies through browser settings. To learn more about cookie management and how to delete cookies from your browser history, visit https://allaboutcookies.org.
