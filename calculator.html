How to use this calculator

Enter the loan amount you plan to borrow, the annual interest rate quoted by your lender, and your preferred tenure in years or months. The EMI, total interest and total payment update instantly as you adjust any field.

The chart shows how much of your total payment is principal (the amount you borrowed) versus interest (the cost of borrowing). Expand the amortization schedule to see exactly how your outstanding balance reduces every month, and print it for your records or to compare against a lender's official quote.
